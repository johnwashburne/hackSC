“The limits of my language mean the limits of my world.” – Ludwig Wittgenstein
Created by John Washburne (Clemson University) and Edward Storey (Georgia Institute of Technology) for their submission at HackSC 2020, Kuratoro is a service driven to make U.S. immigration documentation easier and more accessable to those whose native language is not English. Through the power of machine learning and computer vision, Kuratoro is able to let users read and sign any documents in their native language to then have translated back into English for use by U.S. Customs.
